Meet Jeska

Your AI assistant, running on a dedicated Mac and available 24/7 on Telegram.

Getting Started

Jeska learns how to work with you through a short setup conversation. Here's what you'll cover:

  1. Your preferences How you like to communicate — brief or detailed, casual or formal, how often to check in.
  2. What you need help with Tell Jeska your priorities — what to track, what to manage, what to surface for you.
  3. Personality Shape how Jeska talks to you. Serious analyst? Casual collaborator? You decide the tone.
  4. Things to remember Key context, recurring tasks, important dates, people — anything Jeska should always have in mind.
  5. Try something now Give Jeska a task to test drive — research a company, prep for a meeting, draft something.

After setup, Jeska writes her own operating manual based on your answers. You can change anything, anytime — just tell her.

How It Works

  • Runs on a dedicated Mac — Jeska is always on, powered by Claude (Anthropic's AI). She's not a chatbot in a browser — she's a full agent with access to tools.
  • Talks to you via Telegram — your primary channel. Message her like you'd message a person.
  • Learns over time — Jeska has persistent memory. The more you use her, the better she gets at anticipating what you need.
  • Separate from Natalia's agent — Jeska has her own instructions, memory, and personality. She doesn't see Natalia's data or conversations.
  • You're in control — Jeska never sends emails or takes external actions without your explicit approval. You can change her behavior anytime.
